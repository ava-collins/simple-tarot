## Props

- `cardIndex`: 0-77 index of the drawn card, rendered face-up via the `Card` atom.
- `cardName`, `position`, `reversed`: echoed back from the reading response for the drawn card.
- `text`: the generated reading paragraph for this card.
- `summary`: the overall reading summary.
- `onDonePress`: returns to the account screen.
- `onHistoryPress`: optional, navigates to reading history.

## Responsibilities

- Displays the drawn card's face and its reading text below it.
- Purely presentational — receives resolved reading data as props.
